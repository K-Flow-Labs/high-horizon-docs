# High Horizon Final Operating Boundary and Homepage Summary

> This is not legal advice. It is an operations/planning summary document. Before actual publication, Korean legal/tax matters, mail-order sales business matters, cross-border personal information transfer, and Philippine teacher contract/tax matters must be confirmed with qualified professionals.

## 1. Final Direction

High Horizon should be organized not as a service that directly operates instructional courses like an academy, but as an online lesson marketplace that connects Korean students with independent English teachers in the Philippines.

What the platform does:

- Student signup, Google login, consent collection
- Teacher profile exposure
- Date/time/teacher selection
- Booking request, approval, confirmation
- Payment and refund processing
- Google Calendar/Meet link creation
- Email notifications
- Lesson report/review/dispute handling support
- Operational notices for power outages/internet disruptions

What the teacher does:

- Composes their own lesson content
- Decides their own teaching method
- Conducts lessons
- Writes student feedback
- Provides independent business evidence such as invoices/receipts

Core expression:

```text
High Horizon is not an academy that directly teaches lessons.
It is a platform that connects students with independent teachers
and supports booking/payment/Meet links/dispute handling.
```

```text
High Horizon is not an academy that directly teaches courses.
It is a marketplace platform that connects students with independent teachers
and supports booking, payments, Google Meet access, and dispute handling.
```

## 2. Sales Structure

### 2.1 Flexible ESL Booking

Purpose:

- ESL conversation, speaking practice, specific topic lessons
- The student directly chooses the time slot and teacher each time
- Flexible one-session-at-a-time booking like an ESL platform

Flow:

1. The student chooses a date.
2. The student sees available times and teachers.
3. The student chooses 1:1 or group lesson.
4. The student pays or submits a booking request.
5. 1:1 is confirmed after teacher approval; group can be automatically confirmed within capacity.
6. The student enters the lesson through the Google Meet link.

Homepage copy:

```text
Choose the day, time, and teacher you want.
Flexible ESL booking lets you book one lesson at a time without regular enrollment.
```

```text
Choose the date, time, and teacher that fit your schedule.
Flexible ESL booking lets you book one lesson at a time without a recurring plan.
```

### 2.2 IELTS Short Course Classes

Purpose:

- Short intensive lessons for IELTS preparation
- A course close to a monthly unit, but ending in under 30 days
- Under 30 hours
- Group of up to 4 people or 1:1
- No automatic payment/automatic renewal

Operating limits:

- Course period: under 30 days
- Total lesson hours: under 30 hours
- Group capacity: up to 4 people
- No automatic renewal
- No automatic payment
- After the session set ends, re-enrollment is chosen directly by the student
- Avoid expressions that look like a regular curriculum under the platform name
- Express as teacher-owned or teacher-composed lessons

Homepage copy:

```text
IELTS short courses are session-based lessons composed by teachers.
They end in under 30 days and under 30 hours, and students apply directly without automatic payment or automatic renewal.
```

```text
IELTS short courses are session-based lessons prepared by individual teachers.
Each course stays under 30 days and under 30 teaching hours, with no auto-renewal or recurring billing.
```

## 5. AI Feature Boundary

### 5.1 Free AI Level Check

Keep it. However, do not express it as an official test/placement/guarantee feature.

Definition:

- A reference tool that students use lightly before class to check their current English state
- Results are used only for teacher selection, first lesson preparation, and learning direction consultation
- Results do not mean a score guarantee or an official IELTS band decision

Homepage copy:

```text
Check your current English state first with a simple AI level check,
and use the result as a reference for teacher selection and first lesson preparation.
```

```text
Use a simple AI level check to understand your current English level.
Results are used only as a reference for teacher selection and first-lesson preparation.
```

Caution copy:

```text
AI level check results are for reference only and do not guarantee an official test score or class placement.
```

```text
AI level check results are for reference only and do not guarantee an official test score or class placement.
```

### 5.2 AI Real-Time Transcription/Correction

Do not describe this as a required feature provided in the main High Horizon lessons. Separate it as a teacher SaaS/utility.

Definition:

- A separate tool teachers can use directly during lessons when needed
- Supports transcription, grammar error marking, lesson notes, and lesson report draft generation
- Not a basic lesson feature always provided to students
- Managed as a separate product line from the High Horizon lesson marketplace

Product distinction:

| Product | Role |
|---|---|
| High Horizon Lesson Marketplace | Student-teacher booking, payment, Meet links, refund/make-up |
| Teacher Utility SaaS | Teacher AI transcription, correction, lesson notes, report support |

Homepage copy:

```text
Some teachers may use a separate teacher AI tool to support lesson notes and feedback.
AI does not replace teachers, and the responsible teacher remains responsible for lesson delivery and feedback.
```

```text
Some teachers may use a separate AI utility to support lesson notes and feedback.
AI does not replace the teacher; lesson delivery and feedback remain the teacher's responsibility.
```

## 6. Refund/Cancellation/Make-Up Policy Direction

### 6.1 Individual Lessons

Student cancellation:

- Until 24 hours before lesson start: full refund or credit
- Within 24 hours before lesson start: in principle, no refund
- No-show: in principle, no refund
- If mandatory rules such as statutory withdrawal rights/advertising mismatch are more favorable, apply that standard

Homepage copy:

```text
Individual lessons are processed as a full refund or credit if cancelled up to 24 hours before the lesson starts.
Cancellations within 24 hours or no-shows are generally non-refundable.
```

```text
Individual lessons can be cancelled for a full refund or credit up to 24 hours before the lesson starts.
Cancellations within 24 hours and no-shows are generally non-refundable.
```

### 6.2 IELTS Short Courses

Standard:

- Before start: full refund
- After start: refund based on remaining sessions, excluding sessions already delivered or sessions whose cancellable time has passed
- Apply the standard more favorable to the student among e-commerce withdrawal rights, proportional remaining sessions, and conservative consumer refund standards
- Sessions cancelled due to teacher/platform reasons are not treated as consumed sessions

Homepage copy:

```text
IELTS short courses are fully refundable before they start.
After the course starts, the refund amount is calculated by excluding sessions already provided or sessions whose cancellable time has passed,
using the standard more favorable to the student.
```

```text
IELTS short courses are fully refundable before the first lesson begins.
After the course starts, refunds are calculated by excluding delivered sessions
and sessions that are no longer cancellable, using the standard most favorable to the student.
```

### 6.3 Teacher Absence

Teacher absence or teacher-side cancellation:

- Issue one make-up credit with the same teacher to the student
- If the student wants, allow refund choice for that session
- Default make-up credit validity period: 30 days
- Use only on open times of the same teacher

Homepage copy:

```text
If a lesson is cancelled due to a teacher-side reason, a make-up credit with the same teacher is issued.
If the student wants, they may request a refund for that session.
```

```text
If a lesson is cancelled for teacher-side reasons, the student receives a make-up credit with the same teacher.
The student may request a refund for that session instead.
```

## 7. Baguio Power Outage/Internet Disruption Board

In the Philippines, especially in the Baguio area, power outages/internet disruptions are lesson operation risks. Do not hide this. Manage it with an operations board.

Name candidates:

- Baguio Live Class Status
- Teacher Connectivity Board
- Baguio Power & Internet Board
- Class Connection Status Board

Recommended name:

```text
Baguio Class Status Board
```

Purpose:

- Quickly display power outage/internet disruption in teacher areas
- Clearly guide students on lesson possibility, delay possibility, and make-up handling standards
- Distinguish teacher absence from local infrastructure disruption
- Let operations handle make-up credit/refund decisions consistently

Status values:

| Status | Meaning | Student guidance |
|---|---|---|
| Normal | Normal | Proceed as scheduled |
| Watch | Possibility of power/internet instability | Delay may occur |
| Outage | Power/internet disruption confirmed | Lesson cancellation/make-up handling possible |
| Recovery | Recovering | Proceed after teacher confirmation |

Basic operating rules:

1. When teachers learn of a power outage/internet disruption, they immediately report it to the platform.
2. Operations changes the status board to `Watch` or `Outage`.
3. If confirmed lessons are affected, students receive email/web notifications.
4. If the lesson cannot proceed, process it as `teacher_cancelled`.
5. Provide the student with a make-up credit or refund option.
6. After the disruption ends, return to `Normal` through `Recovery`.

Public scope:

- Can be public: regional status, affected time range, replacement/make-up guidance
- Keep private: teacher personal address, phone number, detailed carrier account information

Homepage/notice copy:

```text
Local power outages or internet disruptions in the Philippines may affect lessons.
High Horizon checks local connectivity status through the Baguio Class Status Board,
and affected lessons are handled according to make-up credit or refund standards.
```

```text
Local power or internet outages in the Philippines may affect live lessons.
High Horizon tracks teacher connectivity through the Baguio Class Status Board,
and affected lessons are handled through make-up credits or refunds.
```

- I compose my own lesson content and lesson method.
- High Horizon supports booking, payment, notifications, Google Meet connection, and dispute handling.
- I issue invoices based on lessons/settlement, not a fixed monthly salary.
- This is not an exclusive contract, and I may also work with other platforms/students.

- I am responsible for Philippine tax filing and payment.
- If a power outage/internet disruption occurs, I report it immediately.
- Delayed reporting, unauthorized absence, or repeated cancellations may affect profile exposure or settlement.
- Courses are opened only under the standards of under 30 days, under 30 hours, and group maximum of 4 people.
- I will not create automatic renewal/subscription-style courses.
- I will use student personal information and lesson records only for lesson provision purposes.
